At the end of October we spent a few days in Southeast Switzerland with Sander, mostly around the Flims and St. Moritz areas. We were extremely lucky with the weather -- perfectly clear skies with zero wind throughout the whole trip. The Swiss landscapes really popped out in this weather, enjoy the photos :-)
